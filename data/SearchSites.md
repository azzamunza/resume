# Job Search Platforms

The following job search platforms are used for searching available positions. All searches are filtered for **Perth, Western Australia** and jobs posted within the **past 14 days (2 weeks)**.

## Search Sites

1. **SEEK Australia**  
   URL: https://www.seek.com.au

2. **Indeed Australia**  
   URL: https://www.indeed.com.au

3. **LinkedIn Jobs**  
   URL: https://www.linkedin.com/jobs

4. **CG Garage**  
   URL: https://cg-garage.com

5. **The Dots**  
   URL: https://the-dots.com

6. **ArtStation Jobs**  
   URL: https://www.artstation.com/jobs

7. **ARIA (Architecture & Related Industries Alliance)**  
   URL: https://aria.org.au

8. **Polycount**  
   URL: https://www.polycount.org

9. **AngelList**  
   URL: https://angel.co

## Search Filters

- **Location Filter:** Perth, Western Australia ONLY
- **Date Filter:** Jobs posted within the past 14 days (2 weeks) ONLY
- **Quantity:** Retrieve as many relevant job listings as available from each platform
- **Data Integrity:** If a platform returns no results, explicitly note this in the metadata sidebar
