Professional Summary

Aaron Munro is an experienced 3D artist and visualisation specialist with over 20 years' experience, including more than 10 years in senior roles focusing on architectural visualisation and product CGI. He is highly proficient in 3ds Max, Corona, V-Ray, Photoshop, and is skilled in creating photorealistic renders for both architecture and infrastructure projects. Aaron's background covers end-to-end project management, 3D modelling, shader and material development, rendering, and post production, working with teams and clients on complex, high-end deliverables. His portfolio demonstrates creative, organised, and efficient work on significant projects for Australian and international clients.
